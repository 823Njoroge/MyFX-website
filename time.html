There are four market sessions

Sydney session

Time(UTC) 10:00PM-7:00AM

This session represents the start of the forex trading day. Activity is generally lower compared to other sessions but can increase when the tokyo session overlaps

Tokyo(Asian) session

Time(UTC)12:00 AM-9:00

The Tokyo session is the second to open and overlaps with the Sydney session for few hours. Major currency pairs like the USD/JPY and EUR/JPY are more active during this time.

London session

Time(UTC):8:00 AM-5:00PM

The London session is one of the most active sessions, as it overlaps with both the Asian and New York sessions.High liquidity and significant trading volumes characterize this session.

New York session

Time(UTC)1:00PM-10:00PM

The New York session overlapswith the London session for several hours, creating high trading activity. It often experiences large market movements as the US market opens
